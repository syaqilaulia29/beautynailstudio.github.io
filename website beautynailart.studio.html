About

welcome to our website, beautynailart.studio is a business that offers beauty services and your fingernail care. Nail art helps maximize one's appearance, for example to be more attractive, fashionable, classy, and many others. Even so, it is not only related to 'painting nails', this nail art business usually provides a series of services to take care of the health of your fingernails. This business was built in 2020 by a young entrepreneur in the beauty sector, namely Syaqila Aulia. in addition to providing treatment services for you, we also offer nail beauty products which are of good quality.
